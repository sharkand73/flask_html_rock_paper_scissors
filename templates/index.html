Playing Instructions

In Rock Paper Scissors, two players will each randomly choose one of three objects: rock, paper, or scissors, without the other player knowing their choice. When the choices are revealed, the winner is chosen according to the following scheme:

  • Scissors defeat paper by cutting it.
  • Rock defeats scissors, because scissors can't cut rock.
  • Paper defeats rock by wrapping itself around it.

Enter your names below to begin. Good luck!
